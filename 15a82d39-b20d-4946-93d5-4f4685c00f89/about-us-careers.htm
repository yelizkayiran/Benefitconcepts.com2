Careers

Over the past two decades, Benefit Concepts has grown into one of the finest employee benefit administration companies in the industry. How have we done it? Our employees. At Benefit Concepts, we encourage employee involvement, open communication and diversity, and we are always looking for talented people who are ready to use their expertise and enthusiasm to help us build on our success.

If you would like to inquire about employment at Benefit Concepts, please complete the form below, or e-mail us at humanresources@benefitconcepts.com. From time to time, we will also list job opportunities below. We invite you to respond to any postings that interest you. We look forward to hearing from you.

Assistant Benefits Administrator

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Producing eligibility reports to claims payers
  • Researching and processing payment exceptions
  • Reconciling client invoices and processing timely payments
  • Processing benefit changes accurately and timely.
  • Process all data entry changes and record all changes in control log.
  • Producing various reports and regular monthly reports.
  • Working in concert with Customer Service to resolve issues.
  • Other duties as requested.

Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent.
  • Minimum of 0-1 years previous office experience.

Critical Success Factors:

  • Strong interpersonal, communication and organizational skills.
  • Knowledge of MS Word helpful.
  • Strong attention to detail and the ability to work as part of a team a must.
  • Ability to perform multiple tasks simultaneously.
  • Responsible for participating in a safe, respectful and harassment free workplace.
