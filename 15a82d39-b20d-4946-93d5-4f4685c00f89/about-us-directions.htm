To Corporate Headquarters

From Boston and Points North

Follow Rte. 95 South through the city of Providence staying in the right lanes. Take the Rte. 195 East exit. Follow Rte. 195 East to Exit 8 (Route 6). Take a left at the end of the exit (Rte. 6 East). Follow Rte. 6 East to the fourth traffic light (Chili's Restaurant on the left and N. E. Health and Racquet on the right). At the traffic light, take a right onto Catamore Boulevard. Follow Catamore to the end (1/3 mile) and take a right onto Risho Avenue. Follow Risho about 200 feet and take a left into the Benefit Concepts parking lot.

At the traffic light, take a right onto Catamore Boulevard. Follow Catamore to the end (1/3 mile) and take a right onto Risho Avenue. Follow Risho about 200 feet and take a left into the Benefit Concepts parking lot.
