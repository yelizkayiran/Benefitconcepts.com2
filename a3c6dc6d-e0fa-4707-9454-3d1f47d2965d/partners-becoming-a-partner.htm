Becoming a Partner

At Benefit Concepts, we know you want to work with an employee benefit administration company that delivers on its promises. We know you need a partner you can trust to provide the same level of superior service to your clients that you do. For two decades, we have helped group insurance brokers and consultants provide their clients with cost-effective employee benefit solutions, and we'd like to do the same for you.

When Benefit Concepts is on your side, you gain direct access to highly experienced benefits professionals you can depend on to provide your clients with a full range of benefit administration services.

If you are a broker or consultant interested in how Benefit Concepts can work for your clients, please call us at 1-800-969-2009, email us at sales@benefitconcepts.com, or Request a Quote.

If you are interested in pursuing a partnership with Benefit Concepts , please contact us by calling us at 1-800-969-2009 or e-mailing us at strategicalliance@benefitconcepts.com.
