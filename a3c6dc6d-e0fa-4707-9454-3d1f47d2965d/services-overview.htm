Services

Our Services can be customized for your company.

When you need a total benefit administration solution,  Benefit Concepts is the answer. From  open enrollment and  ongoing benefit administration to  COBRA compliance and FSA administration, Benefit Concepts is ready to provide you with a complete package of services that can relieve your HR and benefits professionals of many profit-draining, time-consuming duties.

Through the use of proven administrative processes developed over the past two decades and user-friendly Web self-service technology, Benefit Concepts can help you:

  • Serve growing employee populations without increasing staff;
  • Provide your employees with an attractive benefits package featuring tremendous choice and superior service;
  • Reduce the cost of transaction processing;
  • Improve the cycle time of key processes; and
  • Improve information flow to and from managers and employees.

Of course, our services can be customized for your company. You select the level of services that is optimum for your needs, goals and budget.
