Retiree and Direct Billing Administration

In keeping with our comprehensive approach to benefit outsourcing, Benefit Concepts takes care of each step of the retiree and direct billing process. When you have an employee who is continuing their health or other insurance coverage on a self-pay basis (but not under COBRA), you can count of Benefit Concepts to handle everything for you, including:

  • Notification
  • Enrollment
  • Collection and remittance of premiums
  • Eligibility processing
  • Timely terminations
  • Coordination with COBRA

Web-based System

The Benefit Concepts web-based solution allows both participants and clients to access information both efficiently and effectively. Participants can view the status of their account through the Participant Portal. Through the HR Portal, Clients have the ability to view participant information, retrieve any applicable reports, and enter new participants online.
