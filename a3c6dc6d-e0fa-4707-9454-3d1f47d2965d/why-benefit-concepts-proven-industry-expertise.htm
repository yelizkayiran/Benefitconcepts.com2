Proven Industry Expertise

Single Source Vendor

Benefit Concepts is unique in our ability to provide a full range of benefit administration outsourcing services including COBRA, FSA, and Total Enrollment. By selecting a single source provider, you eliminate communication and coordination between vendors. We support your participants both during and after employment.

A Diverse Team of Industry Experts

One of the differentiators that makes Benefit Concepts better than our competitors is the team of experts we have assembled. Our team members are highly skilled and each of them offers unique and diverse skills that complement the others. Also, each recognizes the importance of the process-oriented methods necessary to ensure consistent quality. Our focus is on benefit administration, we are not a payroll company and we do not sell insurance. As a result, we offer unparalleled diversity and depth of experience in developing solutions for our clients.

Unsurpassed Expertise
