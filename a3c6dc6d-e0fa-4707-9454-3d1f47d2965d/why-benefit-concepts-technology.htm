Technology

Faster network communications, greater reliability, and superior overall performance.

Benefit Concepts’ infrastructure is built on high redundancy while maintaining maximum performance. Our N-Tier hosting architecture uses multiple processor load balanced servers and clustering services, protected by a fail-over hardware based firewall system.

Each service within our production environment is scaled independently of others and can be load-balanced individually, thereby creating faster network communications, greater reliability, and superior overall performance. Benefit Concepts is connected to the internet through multiple communication circuits, each routed from different POPs protected by Sprint's four-fiber, bi-directional, line-switching SONET Rings. All communication circuits terminate into Cisco routers, offering full hardware and circuit redundancy. Benefit Concepts guarantees reliability with a 99.9% uptime - all clients are assured of minimal latency and maximum availability.
